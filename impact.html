Impact Vision

Org & Aura aims to build cleaner, safer sanitation systems with measurable social and environmental outcomes through SANDI.

Landfill Contamination Reduction

Safer containment helps keep exposed hygiene waste out of open streams.

Sanitation Worker Safety

Lower direct-contact handling through controlled disposal paths.

Menstrual Dignity

Inclusive infrastructure for users in schools, workplaces, and public areas.

Circular Waste Support

Encourages segregation-ready collection and responsible waste pathways.

Public Infrastructure Upgrade

Improves hygiene standards for high-footfall facilities.
