About Org & Aura

Org & Aura was founded on the belief that waste is not only a challenge, but also an opportunity for meaningful innovation.

Our Story

We identified a critical sanitation gap in menstrual and diaper waste handling and built SANDI to deliver safer, cleaner, and more dignified disposal.

Mission

To develop sustainable waste solutions that improve hygiene, protect ecosystems, and strengthen sanitation infrastructure.

Vision

A world where hygiene waste is managed responsibly, safely, and sustainably.

Sustainability

Solutions that reduce long-term environmental burden.

Innovation

Technology-led systems for practical sanitation challenges.

Safety

Protecting users and sanitation workers across environments.
