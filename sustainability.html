Our Sustainability Approach

At Org & Aura, sustainability is at the core of everything we design. We apply waste reduction, safe containment, environmental protection, and circular-thinking principles in SANDI deployments.

Environmental Goals

  • Reduce landfill burden from unsegregated hygiene waste
  • Improve waste segregation and disposal discipline
  • Promote sustainable sanitation in public and private spaces
  • Support cleaner urban and community environments
